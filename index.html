Earnings & Sector Drivers

Alphabet & AI Rally

Alphabet’s earnings beat and its $85 B AI investment lifted fellow S&P 500 tech giants like Nvidia and Microsoft, powering the index to record highs.

Tesla & Industrial Slump

Tesla—one of the largest S&P 500 constituents—slid after an earnings miss and a “rough quarters” warning, while other S&P 500 industrials like IBM and Honeywell also dropped.

Trade Deals & Macro
